## Intent

Create a quiet, polished bilingual product loop for the right side of the TAWEP home hero.
Show the real answer and report interfaces instead of inventing marketing screens.
The answer workspace provides about two seconds of context; the report remains the
focus and demonstrates score coverage, prioritized actions, interactive grammar
feedback, and the AI Rewrite comparison.

## Assets

- The local TAWEP example session pages provide the answer, report, grammar, and rewrite UI.
- A generated three-character portrait set replaces the current TOEFL-derived professor and student images.

## Customizations

- Show the timed-practice interface reaching 01:00 and pulsing once before the report appears.
- Use a guided spotlight treatment: softened context, sharp focus windows, fluorescent marker sweeps, concise side annotations, and connectors that show how advice is applied.
- Produce separate English and Chinese silent autoplay loops that switch with the site locale, with a poster image and reduced-motion fallback for the home page.

## Notes

- Keep the full product name exactly "TOEFL Academic Discussion Evaluation Project" wherever the expansion appears.
- Do not use TOEFL TPO screenshots, portraits, logos, or other extracted ETS assets.
- Preserve TAWEP as the short brand and keep internal package, database, cookie, and file identifiers unchanged.
- Avoid GIF as the primary asset; deliver browser-friendly MP4 and WebM.
