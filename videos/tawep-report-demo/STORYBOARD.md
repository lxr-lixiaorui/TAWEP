## Video direction

Treat each real TAWEP screen as a product surface, not as a slideshow. Every shot has three independent layers: a softened full interface for context, a sharp spotlight for the active feature, and a concise annotation layer. Use long-tail camera moves, left-to-right fluorescent marker reveals, SVG connector draws, and deliberate holds. Never pan a whole long page, zoom for its own sake, or leave the viewer to infer the feature from an unannotated screenshot.
